Mar 1, 2023

New Identity

Friends, we have amazing news! We are proud to announce that we have revised and updated our corporate identity to better reflect our values and mission.

As a construction corporation, we are always committed to innovation and continuous improvement in our work, and we have decided that our corporate identity should reflect this desire for leadership and development.

We carried out a thorough analysis of our old corporate identity and decided to change it to make it more modern, bright and recognizable. We have created a new logo that better reflects our company and our values.

New logo in monochrome version

We've also updated our colors and typography to better match the new logo and enhance the overall look of our brand identity.

We are proud that our new brand identity is so successful that we can use it on all of our materials, including our website, banner ads, flyers, business cards and more. This will help us to be recognized and be more recognizable among our customers and partners.

New corporate font

We hope you enjoy our new corporate identity as much as we do. We are always striving to improve and improve our work, and we are confident that these changes will allow us to continue to move our company forward. Thank you for staying with us!
